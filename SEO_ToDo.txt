OG Image Quick Guide

  Optimal Size: 1200 x 630 pixels

  This works for Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Discord, Slack.

  ---
  🎨 Easy Creation Methods:

  Option 1: Canva (Recommended - Free)

  1. Go to https://www.canva.com
  2. Click "Create a design" → "Custom size"
  3. Enter: 1200 x 630 pixels
  4. Design your image:
    - Add background color/gradient
    - Add "Widest Life" logo
    - Add headline (60-80px font size)
    - Add tagline (40-50px)
    - Keep text 150-200px from edges
  5. Download as PNG
  6. Compress at https://tinypng.com (aim for <200 KB)
  7. Save to public/og-image.png

  Option 2: Figma (Free)

  Similar process, more professional tool.

  ---
  ✅ Design Best Practices:

  DO:
  - ✅ 1200 x 630 px size
  - ✅ Large, bold text (60px+)
  - ✅ High contrast colors
  - ✅ Include your logo
  - ✅ Simple, clean design
  - ✅ Compress to <200 KB

  DON'T:
  - ❌ Small text (<40px)
  - ❌ Text near edges
  - ❌ Busy/cluttered design
  - ❌ Low contrast
  - ❌ Heavy file size (>500 KB)
